BuzzCheat

Drag the following link to your bookmarks bar. Click it while on a quiz page to show a list of possible results: BuzzCheat

I was curious how BuzzFeed's popular personality quizzes calculate their results. In looking into how they work, I ended up creating a bookmarklet that lets you choose a quiz result and see the answers that would lead to it.

(Note that in many cases, more than one combination of answers can lead to the same result. This uses the first matching answer it finds.)

Examples of quizzes: What State Do You Actually Belong In?; What Font Are You?; Which Classic Rock Band Are You?.

This code makes certain assumptions based on a small sample of quizzes that might not be true for all of them. It may stop working at any time.
